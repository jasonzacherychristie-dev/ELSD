# ELSD 1.0 Vision — Open Source Android

**Working name:** ELSD (Electronic LSD)  
**Platform:** Android (Cardboard-compatible)  
**License (code):** MIT (third-party deps keep Apache-2.0 etc.; see NOTICE)  
**Soul:** Voice-only **TOASTED** perception mixer with **Amy** — spirited checker-orb pilot (lean-in on speech), can go spatial, break the fourth wall, or mute to pure trip.  
**Look bible:** [`SOUL.md`](SOUL.md)  
**Target hardware:** Large flagship (**OnePlus 12 class**) + large-tray phone VR viewer — [`TARGET_HARDWARE.md`](TARGET_HARDWARE.md)

## Lineage (history, not branding)

1. **1998** — *Toward Ultimate Reality — Electronic LSD and Virtual Displays*: video see-through AR, luminance-key virtual theater, electronic psychedelia on consumer gear.  
2. **2025** — Industry caught up on virtual theater + psychedelic VR; long-horizon dome+AR platform remains future work.  
3. **Now** — ELSD 1.0 is a **live perception desk** on a phone: open, DIY, shader banks, keying, world pulse, painter styles, **voice as the only T-bar**.

Public product language **implies** classic desktop-video and demo-scene joy. It does **not** speak protected product names. See [`TRADEMARK_SAFE_LANGUAGE.md`](TRADEMARK_SAFE_LANGUAGE.md).

## Product promise

Treat **live world + keyed media + painter styles + pulse** as busses you mix **TOASTED** — hot, live, wet/dry. Buildings throb. Streams land on windows. Paint walks down the street. **Bounce** is your pilot; when Bounce is quiet, the mix still sings.

## Interaction law

| Law | Rule |
|-----|------|
| **Voice only** | All app commands are spoken |
| **Bounce** | Default 2D spirited cursor; optional 3D spatial orb |
| **Mute** | `mute bounce` → background; FX unchanged |
| **Fourth wall** | Allowed, budgeted, never blocks `clear` |
| **Safety** | `clear` / `sober` / `stop` always win |

## Four filter families

| Family | Role |
|--------|------|
| **KEY** | Luma / chroma key of local or streamed media into the world |
| **PULSE** | Real-world visualizers (edges/brights breathe with audio) |
| **PAINT** | Painter / style-specific looks (shader presets) |
| **LSD** | Classic trip FX (trail, hue, chroma split, kaleido, feedback) |

## Hero demos (ship bar)

1. **Window TV** — luma-key media onto bright region (Bounce can magnet to the bright)  
2. **Gogh Walk** — painter style + city pulse outdoors  
3. **Sky Cinema** — chroma-key sky to a plate/stream  
4. **Quiet TOASTED** — full wet trip with Bounce muted  

## Non-goals for 1.0

Horizon dome hardware, ARCore city meshes, real-time neural style every frame, iOS parity, social network, accounts, touch-first settings UI.

## Ethics (product rules)

- Always honor **clear / sober / stop**  
- Session timer + soft landing (wet → dry)  
- Intensity caps in Gentle / Play profiles  
- No dark-pattern “can’t exit” UX  
- Market as Cardboard-**compatible**, not as an official Google product  
- No forbidden vintage brand names in UI or store copy
