# ELSD 1.0 Vision — Open Source Android

**Working name:** ELSD (Electronic LSD)  
**Platform:** Android (Cardboard-compatible)  
**License (code):** Apache-2.0  
**Spirit:** Amiga 2500 + Video Toaster + VR4, rebuilt as a phone “perception mixer.”

## Lineage

1. **1998** — *Toward Ultimate Reality — Electronic LSD and Virtual Displays* (Jason Z. Christie): video see-through AR, luminance key virtual theater, electronic psychedelia on consumer gear.  
2. **2025** — Medium revisit: Virtual Theater & Electronic LSD marked achieved industry-wide; **Amiga 9000** (dome + AR) is the long horizon.  
3. **Now** — ELSD 1.0 is the **Toaster again**: open, DIY, shader banks, keying, world pulse, painter styles, voice T-bar.

## Product promise

Treat **live world + keyed media + painter styles + pulse** as busses you mix like a Video Toaster. Buildings can throb to audio. A stream can land on a bright window. Van Gogh can melt over the street.

## Four filter families

| Family | Role |
|--------|------|
| **KEY** | Luma / chroma key of local or streamed media into the world |
| **PULSE** | Real-world visualizers (edges/brights breathe with audio) |
| **PAINT** | Painter / style-specific looks (shader presets) |
| **LSD** | Classic trip FX (trail, hue, chroma split, kaleido, feedback) |

## Hero demos (ship bar)

1. **Window TV** — luma-key media onto bright region  
2. **Gogh Walk** — painter style + city pulse outdoors  
3. **Sky Cinema** — chroma-key sky to a plate/stream  

## Non-goals for 1.0

Amiga 9000 hardware, ARCore city meshes, real-time neural style every frame, iOS parity, social network, accounts.

## Ethics (from 1998 paper — product rules)

- Always honor **clear / sober / stop**  
- Session timer + soft landing (wet → dry)  
- Intensity caps in Gentle / Play profiles  
- No dark-pattern “can’t exit” UX  
- Market as Cardboard-**compatible**, not as official Google product
