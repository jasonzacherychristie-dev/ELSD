# Mission Statement — ELSD

## Mission

**Put a live perception mixer in everyone’s hands** — open source, voice-first, and honest about both wonder and risk — so people can remix reality (sight, sound, media, and style) without waiting for a corporate headset or a closed app store silo.

ELSD (Electronic LSD) exists to advance *Ultimate Reality* in the only way that scales: **tools you can fork, study, and improve.**

## Vision

We believe the camera-backed world is the canvas — not a black void filled with polygons.  
Sound can possess buildings. Paint can walk down the street. A keyed stream can become a window. A checker-orb pilot named **Bounce** can aim the mix while you speak.

We build **TOASTED**: a live-desk spirit for perception — wet/dry, banks, program out — without locking the soul of the machine behind a brand name.

Long horizon: dream layers, then **time travel** — the local world reskinned into any era (past or future). Near term: a **Cardboard-compatible Android** app on **large flagship + large-tray phone headset** (OnePlus 12 class) under **MIT**. See `docs/design/TARGET_HARDWARE.md` · `docs/design/TIME_TRAVEL.md`.

## What we ship for

| We optimize for | We refuse |
|-----------------|-----------|
| Real-time, composable FX on live light | Sealed “experience apps” you can’t open |
| Voice as the primary control plane | Finger labyrinths as the only UX |
| Sound-activated hallucinations *of the world* | Visualizers that erase the room |
| Clear / sober / stop always winning | Dark patterns that trap immersion |
| Open docs, open code, clear credits | Silent proprietary cores in the critical path |
| Safety, consent, session limits | Flashy recklessness as default |

## Principles

1. **World first** — Passthrough and keying beat pure synthetic scenes for everyday magic.  
2. **Voice only (by design)** — Bounce and speech are the T-bar; touch is not the mixer.  
3. **Open by default** — MIT for ELSD code and shaders; third-party licenses respected in `NOTICE`.  
4. **Imply heritage, don’t steal brands** — Demo-scene bounce energy and live-desk chrome without restricted product names.  
5. **Hallucinate with consent** — Intensity is a dial; `clear` is sacred.  
6. **Credit the humans and the models** — Jason Z. Christie (research & direction); Grok / xAI (co-implementation). Keep both visible.  
7. **Hobbyist can hold it** — Prefer gear people already own over luxury-only stacks.  
8. **Document as product** — Architecture and soul live in `docs/` so the next mind can continue.

## Non-goals (for now)

- Replacing clinical care or encouraging reckless drug use  
- Building a social network or ad-surveillance platform  
- Requiring proprietary engines for the core path  
- iOS parity before Android 1.0 is real  
- Shipping **DeepDream** before the 1.0 mixer works (**3.0**); **time travel / era rebuild** is **4.0** — see `docs/design/ROADMAP.md`  

## Success looks like

A stranger puts on cheap stereo glass, says a few words, and the room answers — buildings pulse, a window becomes cinema, paint softens the street — then says **clear** and the earth returns.  
They can read the docs, fork the repo, and make it weirder or safer without asking permission.

Keep the mission: **open perception tools for a shared Ultimate Reality.**
